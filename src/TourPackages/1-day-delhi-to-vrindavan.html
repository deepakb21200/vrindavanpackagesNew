Same Day Vrindavan Tour Iternary

1. Morning Departure & Travel (06:00 AM – 09:30 AM)

• 06:00 AM: Pick-up from your chosen location in Delhi, Noida, or Gurgaon.

• 06:00 AM, 09:30 AM: Drive to Vrindavan via the Yamuna Expressway. Make a short comfort stop if needed.

2. Early Morning Darshan (09:30 AM – 12:00 PM)

• 09:30 AM: Arrive in Vrindavan and meet the local guide or driver if applicable.

• 10:00 AM: Shree Banke Bihari Temple: Visit this important temple in Vrindavan for morning Darshan before it closes for the afternoon.

• 11:30 AM: Radha Raman Temple: Visit this historic temple, known for its self-manifested deity (Swayambhu).

3. Mid-day Break & Spiritual Sites (12:00 PM – 03:30 PM)

• 12:00 PM, 01:30 PM: Lunch: Enjoy a traditional vegetarian (sattvik) meal at a local restaurant.

• 01:30 PM, 03:30 PM: Nidhivan/Seva Kunj: Explore these holy forest areas linked to Lord Krishna's divine activities, usually done while major temples are closed.

4. Afternoon & Evening Highlights (03:30 PM – 07:00 PM)

• 03:30 PM: ISKCON Vrindavan (Krishna Balaram Mandir): Visit this well-kept temple complex, timing your visit for the reopening and afternoon Aarti.

• 05:00 PM: Prem Mandir: Arrive at the stunning white marble temple.

• 05:30 PM, 07:00 PM: Prem Mandir Evening Illumination: Spend time enjoying the temple's intricate architecture and watch the impressive light and musical fountain show, which depends on seasonal schedules.

5. Return Journey (07:00 PM – 10:30 PM)

• 07:00 PM: Leave Vrindavan.

• 07:00 PM, 10:30 PM: Drive back to Delhi/NCR via the Expressway.

• 10:30 PM: Drop-off at your original pick-up location, ending the tour.
